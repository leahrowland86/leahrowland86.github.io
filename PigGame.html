  • The game has 2 players, playing in rounds.
  • In each turn, a player rolls the dice as many times as they wish. Each di result gets added to their current score.
  • The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that the current score gets added to their global score. After that, it's the next player's turn.
  • If the player rolls a 1, their current score is wiped out and it's the next player's turn.
  • If the player rolls two 6 in a row, or rolls two 6 at once when playing with two di, their current score AND their global score is wiped out and it's the next player's turn.
  • The first player to reach 100 points or a chosen Final Score on their global score wins the game.
